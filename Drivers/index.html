Proper video and sound drivers are extremely important for 4x4 Evolution to play properly. If you haven't installed a new driver within the last three months, there is a great likelihood that it is now out of date. Please use one of the links below to find and install the latest drivers for your video and sound cards.

If you know the chipset for your video or sound card, it is always best to download the drivers from the makers of the chipset rather than the manufacturer (the brand name). Usually, there is a delay in the amount of time it takes for new video or sound drivers to be sent to and customized by the manufacturer. This means that the drivers from the chipset makers is almost always more current. If you do not know the chipset of your video card, try looking at the reference table of video cards.
 

If you are having some problems running 4x4 Evolution, emailing technical support at support@godgames.com
